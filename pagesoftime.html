Pages of Time

by Ron Dupuis

This work is protected by copyright. It is reproduced here with the author's permission.


Pages of Time was my second vision, not unlike the "flip book" pictures you would draw in the corner of a book.
I was brought back to what visually looked like being caught between pages as they were forming in time.
The right side of the room slowly structured a solid room.
The middle, a comic stretched like on a piece of Silly Putty.
The left side of the room, not yet totally formed.
I saw what could only be explained as the pages of time.
