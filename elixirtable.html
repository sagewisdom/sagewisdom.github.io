NOTE: Sage Goddess Emerald Essence may be used either straight from the bottle or diluted with water. When diluting it with water, its efficiency is slightly reduced, but it is preferable to using it undiluted, because it is far less irritating to the sensitive tissues of the mouth cavity. To compensate for the reduced efficiency, it is necessary to use about fifty-percent more tincture per dose. For each level of effects, the table shows one dose for when the tincture is diluted and one for when it is undiluted.
