After the Invitation
You may experience the awesome Infinite—
If this happens you will think you are dying,
All that dies is pretense—
In this “death” you are prior to the movement of the five senses
Entering the Timeless all pretense is gone
Then one can feel and identify as
The still; open; all directional
Out pouring of radiant compassion—
A perpetual activity that wholly depends on
Perfect rest—
It is conscious Life and acceptance of All


—Anonymous

* Dedicated to the World-Mother who gives of Her Self via Salvia divinorum..
