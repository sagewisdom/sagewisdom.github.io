Gingerbread Men

by Running Eagle

This work is protected by copyright. It is reproduced here with the author's permission.

"Everything and everyone around me, including myself, turned into one big M. C. Escher–like drawing. Sections of the room began to warp into gingerbread men who, marching steadfastly toward the TV, brought everything in their path with them. More and more gingerbread men appeared until they all fit together like a jigsaw puzzle."
