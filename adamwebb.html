INTRODUCTION: Smoking 5x enhanced leaves of Salvia divinorum I had an out-of-body experience and saw half a sun rotating faster/slower depending on the Ravi Shankar music I was listening to. The sun was split in half by the edge of a mirror. At one stage this seemed to be superimposed with a desert landscape with a row of thousands of horses galloping into the distance.



Half a Sun on the mirror,
divided by the edge,
vast pulsing aura,
My vision down there
filled with cascading yellow gold-light.
Rapid anti-clockwise rotation burst
Depends on Ravi in the corner.
Perfect superimposition with immense
desert horizon.
Infinite horses gallop to the edge
of the known.

—Adam Webb
